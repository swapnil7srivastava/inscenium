Inscenium Golden Scene Assets
=============================

Place your test video files in this directory for golden scene testing.

Recommended test assets:
- sample.mp4 (2-5 minute video with clear placement opportunities)
- trailer.mp4 (movie trailer with various shots)
- sports.mp4 (sports content with dynamic scenes)
- product_demo.mp4 (static product shots)

Video requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 video, AAC audio)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Duration: 30 seconds to 10 minutes
- Frame rate: 24fps, 30fps, or 60fps
- Content should include clear surfaces suitable for placement

The test suite will automatically use mock/stub implementations
when video files are not present, ensuring CI/CD compatibility.

For actual testing with ML models:
1. Set MOCK_ML_MODELS=false in your environment
2. Ensure all required models are downloaded
3. Run: make golden
